Budget products ($0–10) rely on Jojoba Oil, Vitamin E as workhorses. Mid-tier ($10–25) adds Hyaluronic Acid and Niacinamide as differentiators. Premium ($25–50) brands consistently feature Bakuchiol and Squalane to justify price points.

  • Essential baseline: Rosehip Seed Oil, Vitamin E, Jojoba Oil
  • Mid-tier differentiator: Hyaluronic Acid, Niacinamide (+$8–12 premium)
  • Premium justifier: Bakuchiol, Squalane (+$20–30 premium)
3. Winning Formula

BSR top-20% products share 3 ingredients absent from bottom-20%: Bakuchiol, Sea Buckthorn, and Pomegranate Seed Oil. High-rating products (4.5+) consistently feature Squalane and Ferulic Acid.

Recommended formulation direction: Rosehip Seed Oil (base) + Vitamin E + Hyaluronic Acid + Niacinamide + Bakuchiol — targets the $28–38 price point with demonstrable differentiation.

Three dominant brand archetypes: K-Beauty specialists (COSRX, Some By Mi) owning the Mid tier, Natural/Organic specialists (Trilogy, Kosmea) dominating Premium, and Mass market brands competing in Budget.

Market gap: No strong player in the $20–28 range combining K-Beauty formulation with clear "clean beauty" certification marketing.
